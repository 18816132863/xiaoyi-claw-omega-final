------------------------------------------------------------
  3. 数据库表结构
------------------------------------------------------------

tasks 表字段:
- id, user_id, task_type, goal, payload_json
- trigger_mode, status, schedule_type, run_at, cron_expr
- timezone, next_run_at, last_run_at, attempt_count
- max_attempts, retry_backoff_seconds, timeout_seconds
- idempotency_key, last_error, created_at, updated_at

task_runs 表字段:
- id, task_id, run_no, workflow_thread_id, checkpoint_id
- current_step, total_steps, status, started_at, ended_at
- error_text, retry_after, created_at

task_events 表字段:
- id, task_id, task_run_id, event_type, event_payload, created_at

task_steps 表字段:
- id, task_run_id, step_index, step_name, tool_name
- input_json, output_json, status, started_at, ended_at
- error_text, idempotency_key, created_at

tool_calls 说明:
系统使用 task_steps 表记录工具调用，而非独立的 tool_calls 表。
